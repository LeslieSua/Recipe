Onigiri Recipe

Ingredients

  • Japanese Short-Grain Rice
  • Nori Seaweed
  • Optional fillings

Cooking steps

  1. Cook short-grain rice with arice cooker or pot over the stove
  2. Using a mold or your hands, make rice balls
  3. Salt rice balls for food safety
  4. Fillings are optional, stuff the filling within the rice ball
  5. Wrap the rice ball with nori seaweed

Onigiri Fillings

  • Shake (Salted Salmon)
  • umeboshi (Japanese pickled plum)
  • okaka (Bonito flakes moistened with soy sauce)
  • kombu (Simmered kombu seaweed)
  • tuna mayo (canned tuna with Japanese mayonnaise)
  • mentaiko or tarako (Spicy salted cod roe)
  • furikake (rice seasonings to sprinkle all over)
